# Contributor Covenant (summary)

Our project follows the Contributor Covenant to foster an open, respectful, and safe environment.

Standards
- Be respectful and considerate.
- Do not harass or discriminate.
- Give thoughtful, constructive feedback.

Enforcement
- Maintain civility in discussions and PRs.
- Report violations to repository maintainers.
- Maintainers may warn or remove contributors for misconduct.

This project values contributors of all backgrounds and aims to uphold a positive, collaborative culture.
